Which Lesson is Best?

Our Lesson Types

Private Swim Lessons

One-on-one instruction for students ages three months through adult. Best option for accelerated learning.

Semi-Private Swim Lessons

For students ages 2.5 to 13. Interact with another student of similar age and ability.

Parent & Me Lessons

Also known as "Mommy and Me," giving parents the opportunity to be hands-on with their little one.

Stroke Clinic

Advance swimming techniques while in a group setting. Focus on proper technique over speed.

Summer Swim Camps

Building Swimming Skills All Summer Long

If your child is between the ages of 5 to 14 and can swim two lengths across a 25 yard pool unassisted, consider enrolling them in our summer swim camps.

During the day, students will work with our highly qualified instructors on stroke technique, breathing, starts and turns, along with speed work.
